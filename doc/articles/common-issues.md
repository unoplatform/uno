# Troubleshooting

The Uno Platform features and support are constantly evolving, yet you may encounter some of the issues while building your application, for which answers and details are available below.

[!include[getting-help](includes/getting-help.md)]

A better resource for high-level questions about Uno Platform is the [general FAQ](xref:Uno.Development.FAQ).

## Uno Platform Studio

For issues related to Uno Platform Studio productivity tools:

### Hot Reload

When using Hot Reload, ensure you first review the [supported features](xref:Uno.Features.HotReload#features), [supported features per OS](xref:Uno.Features.HotReload#supported-features-per-os), and [supported features per platform](xref:Uno.Features.HotReload#supported-features-per-platform).

If issues persist, additional troubleshooting information is available in [this section](xref:Uno.Features.HotReload#troubleshooting).

### Hot Design®

For Hot Design® issues:

- Review [current known issues](xref:Uno.HotDesign.Troubleshooting)
- Ensure you are [signed in with your Uno Platform account](xref:Uno.GetStarted.Licensing)
- Verify your platform is supported (iOS, Android, WebAssembly, Skia desktop targets)
- Check [Hot Reload support](xref:Uno.Features.HotReload) for your OS, IDE, and target platform

### Licensing and Sign-In

If you're having trouble with Uno Platform Studio licensing or sign-in:

- See [Sign in with Uno Platform](xref:Uno.GetStarted.Licensing) for setup instructions
- Verify your account at [platform.uno/my-account](https://platform.uno/my-account)
- Check license assignment for team subscriptions
- Contact [info@platform.uno](mailto:info@platform.uno) for licensing support

### Studio Feedback

To report issues or provide feedback on Uno Platform Studio tools:

- Visit the [Uno Platform Studio GitHub repository](https://github.com/unoplatform/studio)
- Use the Feedback menu in Hot Design® (Help > Feedback)
- See [Providing Feedback for Uno Platform Studio](xref:Uno.Platform.Studio.Feedback)
