### Headline findings

- **The bulk is faithful.** 77 files are complete 1:1 ports; 72 are graded *Faithful 1:1* against the C++ member order and logic. The RichTextServices contract, BlockLayout node tree, element-model `.TextContainer` partials, and value-type/enum leaves are near-verbatim.
- **Low-completeness files are deliberate scaffolds, not defects.** The `*.BlockLayout.skia.cs` `GetBaselineOffset` slots, `InlineUIContainer.BlockLayout`, `UIElement.BlockLayout` `LayoutStorage` shim, and `TextCollapsingCharacters` (ellipsis) are Stage-4/6 placeholders that throw `NotSupportedException` by design — reviewers confirm they match the plan's staged deferrals.
- **The control layer (Cluster G) is the least complete** (mean 75%): `RichTextBlock`/`RichTextBlockOverflow` are mid-reshape onto the BlockLayout seam, and the linked-overflow chain is the largest net-new behavior (intended, per the plan's Stage 9 deferral).
- **Bridge files read as "structural deviations / reimagined" by design.** `SkiaTextLine`, `ParagraphDrawingContext`, `TextFormatting` (snapshot POCO), `TextBlock.TextContainer` intentionally reshape C++ onto Uno's `ParsedText`/DP model rather than copying it — the plan's explicit Strategy-B bridge.

### Out-of-scope categories (brief notes)

- **SamplesApp (7 pages / 14 files, net-new):** correctly attributed (`[Sample("RichTextBlock", …)]`, auto-discovered) and cover Basic / Hyperlinks / Programmatic / Selection / TextHighlighters / TextLayout / Typography. **Deviation:** hard-coded colors, **zero `{ThemeResource}`** — the grey helper-text/border chrome won't adapt to dark theme. **Likely bug:** `RichTextBlock_Hyperlinks.xaml` sets a "GitHub" link's `NavigateUri` to `github.com/nicehash` (almost certainly meant `github.com/unoplatform/uno`).
- **Generated stubs (4 files):** all compliant with `/winui-port` Phase 6 — attribute/`#if`-only (members now hand-declared collapsed to `// Skipping already declared` comments); **no implementation leaked**. `RichTextBlock` still `[NotImplemented]` for `IsColorFontEnabled, OpticalMarginAlignment, TextLineBounds, TextReadingOrder, BaselineOffset`.
- **Design docs (4):** `richtextblock-port-plan.md` (the source-of-truth staged blueprint) + Stage 5/6/9 handoff/manifest notes. Internal engineering docs; no user-facing/ private-tracker references.

### How to read the metrics

1. **Completeness %** — share of the C++ source's members/logic faithfully represented (100% = complete or correct net-new). &nbsp; 2. **Uno-specific** — None→Heavy, with intent. &nbsp; 3. **Accuracy** — member ordering + inner-logic fidelity vs C++. &nbsp; 4. **Not ported** — commented/`#if false`/`#if !HAS_UNO`/TODO, tagged *[intended]* vs *[gap]*. &nbsp; 5. **Un-portable** — deliberately dropped low-level APIs. &nbsp; 6. **Summary** — the human-facing verdict.
